Popular Destinations


Visit

AGRA,INDIA

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Taj Mahal is an iconic white marble mausoleum built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.


Visit

NEW DELHI,INDIA

: The capital city of India, Delhi, is steeped in history and culture. Major attractions include the Red Fort, India Gate, Qutub Minar, Lotus Temple, and the Jama Masjid.


Visit

DARJILING,INDIA

Known for its picturesque tea gardens, Darjeeling offers breathtaking views of the Himalayas and is famous for the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.


Visit

VARANASI,INDIA

Considered one of the oldest inhabited cities in the world, Varanasi is a sacred city for Hindus. It's known for its ghats (steps leading to the river), temples, and spiritual significance.


Visit

GOA,INDIA

Known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese-influenced architecture, Goa is a popular tourist destination.


Visit

AMRITSAR,INDIA

The holiest city for Sikhs, Amritsar is home to the Golden Temple, a stunning Sikh shrine.


Visit

LADAKH,INDIA

Leh-Ladakh, Jammu and Kashmir: This region, located in the northernmost part of India, is known for its breathtaking landscapes, high-altitude deserts, and Tibetan Buddhist monasteries.


Visit

KERALA,INDIA

Kerala Backwaters, Kerala: The backwaters of Kerala offer a unique and serene experience, with a network of canals, lakes, and rivers surrounded by lush greenery.
